Generalized Low Rank Parity Check (GLRPC) codes are a class of GF(q)-linear rank metric matrix codes generated from LRPC matrices together with a 3-tensor over GF(q). As a generalization of LRPC codes, they allow for poly-time probabilistic decoding. More info. about GLRPC can be found at https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.02053.
